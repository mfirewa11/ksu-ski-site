The Mountain

Why Aspen / Snowmass

Aspen and Snowmass represent the pinnacle of American ski culture. Four interconnected mountains — Aspen Mountain, Aspen Highlands, Buttermilk, and Snowmass — offer a combined 5,527 acres of skiable terrain and enough variety to keep any group busy for a week without repeating the same run twice. Snowmass alone, at 3,332 acres, is one of the largest ski areas in North America.


Aspen Mountain's steep, perfectly groomed frontside runs are some of the most satisfying carving terrain in Colorado. Aspen Highlands delivers Highland Bowl — a legitimate backcountry experience accessed via a 45-minute bootpack — that draws expert skiers and snowboarders from around the world. Buttermilk is a haven for beginners and terrain park riders. Snowmass handles everything in between, with massive beginner zones, long cruising runs, and expert glades that reward exploration.


Off the mountain, the town of Aspen is in a category of its own for après ski. World-class dining, historic bars that have been part of ski culture for decades, and an energy that is uniquely Aspen. For the KSU alumni group ski trip, Aspen/Snowmass delivered on every dimension — terrain, town, and the biggest group we have ever brought to a mountain. It set the standard for every trip to come.

Where We Stayed

Viewline Resort Snowmass

Autograph Collection · Marriott

The Viewline Resort Snowmass is the pinnacle of mountain lodging — a slopeside Autograph Collection hotel sitting directly on the Snowmass ski area with ski-in/ski-out access from your door. Contemporary mountain design with a 1960s flair, floor-to-ceiling windows, and views that make it hard to get out of bed in the morning. For the KSU alumni group, it put everyone steps from the lifts and steps from each other.

⛷

Ski-In / Ski-Out

Direct slope access — click into your bindings and go. No shuttle, no walking. The mountain starts at the hotel door.

♨

Viewline Spa by RAKxa

The only ski-in/ski-out spa in Snowmass. Eucalyptus steam rooms, oxygen recovery lounge, hot stone massage, HydraFacial, and a heated outdoor pool with mountain views.

🍸

On-Site Dining & Bars

Stark's Alpine Grill, Bunny's Social Club, The Flower Shop, and the Viewline Lobby Bar — everything the group needed without leaving the building.

🏔

Slope-View Rooms

Rooms look directly onto the Snowmass mountain. Wake up, check conditions, and be on the lift in minutes.
